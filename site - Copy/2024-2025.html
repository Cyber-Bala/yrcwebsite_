Estrella' 24

We had our Investiture Ceremony, Estrella, during which the board and core members were formally appointed to take up their roles. We pledged to uphold the values of the Youth Red Cross and to lead the club with dedication. United by a shared vision, we officially commenced our journey, committed to driving the club's success in every endeavor we undertake.

19/10/2024

Agnirodh

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to the dedicated firefighters and trainers of AGNIRODH for equipping our non-teaching staff with essential fire safety and first aid skills. Together, we are now prepared to respond to emergencies with confidence and competence.

21/10/2024

Thithikkum Deepavali

We had a rewarding experience at the Thithikum Deepavali event by the Gold Heart Foundation, celebrating with over 1,500 children. It was an honor to volunteer, spreading joy and contributing to brighten the lives of underprivileged kids. We remain dedicated to making a difference and bringing light to those in need.

27/10/2024

Christmas Celebration

The Youth Red Cross REC celebrated Christmas with a vibrant event featuring games, performances, and a visit from Santa Claus. Students shared laughter, music, and festive cheer, while cake distribution symbolized unity and joy. By combining celebration with compassion, the Youth Red Cross extended support to those in need and created a memorable experience for all participants.

21/12/2024

Pongal Celebration

The Youth Red Cross of Rajalakshmi Engineering College celebrated Pongal with students and faculty, organizing fun activities like Rangoli, Uriyadi, and Tug of War. The event fostered unity, joy, and teamwork while honoring tradition, reflecting the club’s commitment to bringing people together and creating memorable experiences for the community.

11/01/2025

Vaazh 2.0 - Orphanage Visit

The Youth Red Cross of Rajalakshmi Engineering College organized “Vaazh 2.0”, an orphanage visit to Annaikkum Karangal, to spend quality time with the children. Volunteers engaged in games and sports, sharing laughter and joy while fostering connection and compassion. The initiative reflected the club’s spirit of empathy, service, and creating memorable experiences for the community.

21/12/2024

Eco-Trim Lake Cleaning

The Youth Red Cross of Rajalakshmi Engineering College, in collaboration with the Social Work Team Trust, successfully conducted the “Eco Trim” lake cleaning initiative. The dedicated volunteers worked diligently to restore the cleanliness of the lake, raise environmental awareness, and inspire a sense of responsibility toward the preservation of natural resources.

09/02/2025

Wellness Waves

To mark World Health Day, the Youth Red Cross REC organized “Wellness Waves,” promoting holistic well-being through interactive activities. Students engaged in physical challenges, mind games, and problem-solving exercises, highlighting the importance of balancing body, mind, and spirit. This initiative inspired the campus community to adopt healthy habits and prioritize overall wellness.

05/04/2025

Ikshana 3.0 Eye Check-up Camp

The Youth Red Cross REC organized “Ikshana 3.0”, an eye care camp for teaching and non-teaching staff members. Participants received comprehensive eye examinations and were guided through simple eye exercises, emphasizing the importance of preventive eye care. This initiative strengthened the bond between the Youth Red Cross and the campus community while promoting the well-being of every member.

17/04/2025

Festora

The Youth Red Cross REC commemorated Independence Day with a visit to Samarpana, an orphanage for individuals with intellectual challenges. The event included hoisting the national flag, sharing a special meal, and extending donations, reflecting a combination of patriotism and service. Through these actions, the Youth Red Cross reaffirmed its commitment to empathy and social responsibility.
